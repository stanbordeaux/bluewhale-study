Collaborations

Dr Deb Thiele, Whale Ecology Group, Deakin University, Warrnambool, VIC, Australia: acoustic behaviour of whale and dolphin species, baleen whale ecology & habitat studies -
www.deakin.edu.au/

Dr Luciana Moller, Marine Mammal Research Group, Macquarie University, NSW, Australia: stock identification of blue whales -
www.gse.mq.edu.au/Research/mmrg/Staff/Staff.htm

West Australian Blue Whale Project, WA, Australia: photo-ID matching, migration, acoustics, feeding habitat studies, satellite tagging -
www.cmst.curtin.edu.au/research/wa_bluewhales/index.html, www.cwr.org.au/

John Calambokidis and Greg Schorr, Cascadia Research, Olympia, WA, USA: foraging and feeding, and acoustic behaviour of blue whales using suction-cup digital tags - www.cascadiaresearch.org

Dr Nick Gales, Dr Simon Jarman and Dr Toby Jarvis, Australian Antarctic Division, TAS, Australia: medium duration movements of blue whales using satellite tags, genetic analysis of prey species from faecal samples, krill hydroacoustics -
www.aad.gov.au

SARDI Aquatic Sciences: blue whale habitat as an indicator of oceanic productivity -
www.sardi.sa.gov.au/
